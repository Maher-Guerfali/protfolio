Project Overview
Welcome to Elkarrita, your one-stop online shop inspired by the charm of my country traditional market carts! My platform features a diverse range of products, from unique 3D printables crafted by my brother and me (yes, we actually made those!), to cutting-edge virtual reality gifts that elevate your tech experience. Explore our delicious homemade granola, a labor of love created with my friend (because, yes, I can cook good granola!). What sets Elkarrita apart is my innovative XR feature, allowing you to view select 3D models in real time using your camera or a VR headset—perfect for examining details before making a purchase. Built with React, Tailwind, MongoDB, Three.js, and Needle.js from Unity, our website is designed for seamless browsing and a delightful shopping experience. Discover the magic of Elkarrita today!

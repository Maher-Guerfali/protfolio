About Me

I’ve been passionate about computers since the age of 5, when I first got my hands on my mom’s computer. Growing up in Tunisia, I would save up to buy internet cards—$3 for 30 minutes of internet access! Even at my young age, I was eager to explore the online world. By the time I was in school, I became a "script kiddo," fascinated by cybersecurity, game hacks, and the darker sides of the internet.In high school, I expanded my skills, learning HTML and cloning websites, creating landing pages, and experimenting with web development. Fast forward to 2019, when I was 21, I started freelancing, earning money from web development, video game creation, and 3D modeling. My passion for technology evolved into a career.After earning my computer engineering degree in 2023, I became the main developer for the Pingoos NFT collection. I was responsible for all aspects of the project, from building the website to developing the video games. Today, I’m focused on a more professional industry as a full-stack XR developer. Thanks to my engineering background and extensive experience, I’m confident in my ability to tackle any programming or technical challenge, always pushing the boundaries of what I can create.
